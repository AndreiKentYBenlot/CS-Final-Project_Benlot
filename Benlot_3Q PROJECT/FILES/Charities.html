Around the world, many people are living with contaminated waters that affect their day-to-day life. We would like to share a few charities that also follow the same goal, solving water waste management. Donating even a small amount could help the charities raise enough money to establish more programs in the future. Please feel free to check the charities!

- H2GO! Team

1. Generosity.org

A water organization called Generosity.org is dedicated to resolving the clean water dilemma in impoverished nations. The organization has also completed over 800 water wells in 20 different nations.

2. Pure Water for the World

Pure Water for the World equips families and children throughout the Caribbean and Central America with the knowledge and resources they need to create sustainable sanitation, hygiene, and water solutions.

3. Blood: Water

A water company called Blood:Water collaborates with grassroots African organizations to provide clean water and HIV/AIDS assistance to one million individuals across 12 nations.
