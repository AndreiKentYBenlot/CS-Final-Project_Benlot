The website H2GO! is a website that aims to provide solutions and help to water-related problems. Contaminated water is a huge problem in the Philippines, and it is a risk to everyone. These waters may hold dangerous bacteria and virus such as the Polio virus, which affected many people especially the ones living near contaminated waters. It also aims to solve the problems in the environment like the marine life. Various links are posted in the website for charities raising awareness to the concerns.
