About Us!

Since 2015, the debut of the organization, we had created over 50 programs worldwide. We have members from USA, Mexico, Vietnam, and many more. We are unified with one goal in mind, saving our home little by little.

More about the Founder

The organization was founded Sir Ronaldo Quezon from the Philippines. As a kid, he liked watching the river every day after class. However, he noticed that the river is getting dirty, and many trash are getting thrown in the river. This encouraged him to study in the field of hydrology. With the help of his professors, he was able to organize a small group. Two years later, he established an organization that is now known as the "H2GO!".
